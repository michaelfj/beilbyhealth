Booking guide

To book a consultation with Michael. There are two ways:

Clickbook

Our online appointment booking service.

A visual guide is available to walk you through the booking process. You can open the mobile or PC version.

Hint: You can open the mobile guide on your PC or tablet and use it to guide the booking process on your mobile. The mobile site is much easier to use.

Click here to access Clickbook Mobile. You can use the BOOK NOW link at the top of the page in future.

If you are using a wide-screen device (PC, laptop or tablet), you can use the BOOK NOW link at the top of the page in future but for now click here.

Note: in the mobile version, you sign up first as a new user and create a password before you search for "beilbyhealth", while on the wide screen version you do the search first then sign up before your appointment booking is accepted.


Mobile service

Using your mobile call or SMS 0404 090 407 to arrange an appointment. Leave your name, number or email, time and day you would like, length of consult wanted and concession status. We will get back to you ASAP with some options.

Email contact

If you have questions, you can send an enquiry to Michael using the email michael@beilbyhealth.com.au.
